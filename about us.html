Guidelines for Journals Publishers

They are supposed to

  1. Strictly obey OARS guidelines and also insist his counterpart [i.e. fellow editor] follow the same.
  2. Make the associated editors and journal aware regarding rights and responsibilities being a member of OARS
  3. Offer justified concrete backing to editors to enable them to obey OARS guidelines

  1. Make an agreement reflecting correlation among publisher, editor and other associates.
